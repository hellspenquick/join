FAQs

Should I book everything in advance?

Popular times benefit from early bookings. Flexible trips can keep options open in off-peak periods.

How much driving per day is reasonable?

Plan conservative distances with rest breaks — especially on unfamiliar or remote roads.

Can I rely on walk-in stays?

Varies by region and season; carry backup options and check availability ahead.
