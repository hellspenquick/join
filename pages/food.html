FAQs

Is vegetarian/vegan food easy to find?

Cities provide many options; regional areas vary. Check menus in advance.

Do markets accept cards?

Many do, but carrying a small amount of cash can help.

How far are food trails from cities?

Some are within an hour; others require longer drives. Confirm opening days.
