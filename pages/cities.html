FAQs

Which city is best for first-time visitors?

Any major city offers a mix of culture and outdoor areas. Choose based on interests and season.

How about public transport cards?

Cities offer card or app systems; check local transport sites for details.

Are day trips common?

Yes. Many cities provide access to coastal paths, wineries, or parks within a few hours.
