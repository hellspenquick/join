Outdoor Safety

Wear suitable footwear, carry water, and check weather.

  • Stay on marked paths

Beach Safety

Swim between flags and heed lifeguard advice where available.

  • Avoid rips
  • Learn signage

Sun & Heat

Use sun protection and hydrate regularly.

  • Plan breaks
  • Carry electrolytes if needed

Driving

  • Obey speed rules
