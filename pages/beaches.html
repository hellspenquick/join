FAQs

Can I swim anywhere?

Only where it’s allowed and conditions are safe. Follow local signage and lifeguard guidance.

When are beaches busiest?

Weekends, holidays, and warm seasons draw more visitors. Arrive early for parking and space.

What gear should I bring?

Sun protection, water, and appropriate footwear. For snorkeling areas, check tour guidelines.

Are there family facilities?

Many popular areas provide amenities like showers, playgrounds, and picnic spots.
