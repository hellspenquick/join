FAQs

Are dogs allowed?

Rules vary. Many parks restrict pets to protect wildlife. Check official guidance for each park.

Do I need a 4WD?

Some tracks require it; others are sealed. Review park notes and signage.

Can I have a campfire?

Only where permitted and often season-dependent. Use provided facilities if available.

How do I learn track difficulty?

Most parks publish grades and estimated times. Choose tracks suitable for your group.
